Objective

Grow your plant through 5 stages across 50 tiles.

Land on any Rain Tile (42, 44, 46, 48) and the rain waters your plant — you WIN! 🏆

Each stage requires you to collect a key item. Miss the buy tiles and you pay a mandatory penalty at the end of that stage!

🏺

Stage 1 — Pot (Tiles 1–10)

⭐ Tile 3 — Land here for a FREE pot! No money deducted.

☠️ Tile 9 — Danger! Lose your pot → Game Restarts!

⚠️ Missed Tile 3? By the end of Stage 1 you must buy a pot:
  • Easy: ₹60  |  Medium: ₹120  |  Hard: ₹150

💡 Can't afford it? Journey restarts with ₹500!

🟢 EASY MODE

💰 Starting money: ₹800
⚠️ Mandatory penalty: ₹60 per missed item
☠️ Danger tiles (9, 43, 49): Go back 3 tiles only — no restart!
💸 Tile 13 (Sell Pot): Go back to Tile 1 — money kept!
🎲 Dice: Normal 1–6

👶 Best for: New players learning the game!

🟡 MEDIUM MODE

💰 Starting money: ₹500
⚠️ Mandatory penalty: ₹120 per missed item
☠️ Danger tiles: Full restart! Money resets to ₹500.
💸 Tile 13 (Sell Pot): Get ₹200 but full restart!
🎲 Dice: Normal 1–6

⚖️ Best for: Balanced, classic gameplay experience!

🔴 HARD MODE

💰 Starting money: ₹300 only!
⚠️ Mandatory penalty: ₹150 per missed item
☠️ Danger tiles: Full restart! Money resets to ₹300.
💸 Tile 13 (Sell Pot): Only ₹100 + full restart!
🎲 Dice: 1–4 only (slower, more tile landings!)
⚡ Weather Events every 5 turns affect BOTH players:
  ☀️ Heatwave → lose ₹50  |  🌧️ Flood → back 2 tiles
  🐛 Pests → lose soil & seeds  |  💨 Wind → forward 2 tiles
  ❄️ Frost → lose ₹40  |  🌈 Perfect Day → earn ₹80

🏆 TOURNAMENT MODE

🌟 Best of 3 Rounds — first to win 2 rounds is Champion!
💰 Starting money: ₹500 per round
🏅 Round winner earns 10% of their money as bonus for next round!
🥇 Round winner goes first in the next round!

🃏 Power Cards — each player gets 1 random card:
  🛡️ Shield — Auto-blocks ONE restart
  💰 Loan — Borrow ₹150 instantly
  🎯 Precision — Jump exactly 6 tiles forward
  🔄 Swap — Swap positions with opponent!
